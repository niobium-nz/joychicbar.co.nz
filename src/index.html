Our Menu

Welcome to a bar that perfectly combines Eastern charm with a harbour vibe.

As a top-notch cocktail bar nestled along Auckland's picturesque waterfront, we whip up Eastern-inspired drinks that are truly choice. Drawing on Eastern culture, we use primo local ingredients and classic Eastern spices to craft cocktails with unique flavors. Whether you're after a refreshing brew based on floral teas or a rich concoction that balances the five flavors, we guarantee a taste experience that's hard to beat.
